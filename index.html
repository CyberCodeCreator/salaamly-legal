1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer (individual) of the Salaamly mobile application ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about users ("user", "you") when using our application.

We are committed to ensuring transparency in data processing in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide a high-quality educational service.

  • Personal Data: Name, username, and email address. Used for profile creation, synchronizing learning progress, and identification.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Non-identifiable information about your device (model, OS version) and usage statistics via third-party analytics.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Apple/Google. We do not have access to your credit card or bank details.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party tools:

  • Authentication: Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for Firebase.
  • App Stores: Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

4. Age Restrictions

Salaamly is intended for adults and teenagers (13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If discovered, such data will be immediately deleted.

5. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access and Correction: Update info in your profile.
  • Erasure: Delete account/data via app settings or email.
  • Opt-out: We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of an account deletion request.

7. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app implies consent to changes.
